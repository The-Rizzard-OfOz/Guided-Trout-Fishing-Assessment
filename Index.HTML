New Zealand has some of the most beautiful scenery and trout fishing of anywhere in the world. Over 30 rivers and streams in the Taupo district make this part of New Zealand a fishing paradise. John Hamilton and his guides can take you on an experience of a lifetime. Professionally guiding in the Taupo district for 15 years, John knows some of the best out-of-the-way locations where you can catch the trout of your dreams.
